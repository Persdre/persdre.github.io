# The Need to Read

Paul Graham's essay [The Need to Read](http://www.paulgraham.com/read.html) argues that reading and writing are fundamentally interconnected skills essential for deep thinking.

## Reading Teaches Writing

Consuming written material develops writing ability, which cannot be replaced by technological alternatives.

## Writing as Thinking Tool

"Writing is not just a way to convey ideas, but also a way to have them." Writers discover novel insights during the composition process itself.

## The Reading-Writing-Thinking Connection

"You can't think well without writing well, and you can't write well without reading well." This creates a necessary chain for intellectual development.
